# **App Name**: Shalom Connect

## Core Features:

- AI Health Orientation Tool: An AI assistant that uses symptoms as reasoning to tool users toward the most appropriate clinical specialty or health service.
- Multi-Specialty Medical Grid: Responsive grid display of 14+ medical specialties including cardiology, psychology, and pediatric care with interactive card hovering.
- Conversion-Optimized Pricing Cards: High-contrast dynamic table comparing Individual, Family, and Corporate plans with automated pricing per additional life calculation.
- Five-Step Performance Funnel: Segmented 5-step data capture process (Personal, Address, Dependents, Terms, Confirmation) optimized for high Meta and Google Ads performance.
- Smart Referral Tracking: Capture system to parse and store 'ref' URL parameters like 'MARCELO-LIMA' to maintain seller partner attribution and CRO analytics.
- Document & Terminal Access: One-click digital term access and simplified inclusion management for legal transparency and onboarding.
- Telemedicine Hub: Specific focus module for 24h attendance, featuring digital prescriptions and medical certificate indicators.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Deep Trust Blue (#1F3B77) for health authority and security.
- Background: Clean Healthcare Gray (#F2F4F7), ensuring focus remains on high-converting elements.
- Accent: Deep Sky Blue (#1479A5) for supportive interface elements, used to highlight secondary medical categories.
- CTA Strategy: High-performance Emerald Green (#16A34A) is used exclusively for primary call-to-action buttons to maximize lead conversion.
- Headline and body font: 'Poppins' for a contemporary, precise, and avant-garde aesthetic across all technical copy.
- Mobile-first conversion layout with a pulsating floating action button (FAB) for starting registration and cards grouped by user needs.
- Soft CSS transitions for price changes and a pulse effect on green 'Começar Cadastro' buttons to increase visibility for paid traffic visitors.
